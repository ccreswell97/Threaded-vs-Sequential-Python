Did You Know

We have a 15% higher graduate employment rate

Thanks to our practical focus and strong industry ties, 15% more Lincoln alumni find employment within the first year of graduation, compared to graduates from other New Zealand universities. 

Learn more about Lincoln

We are the 15th best small university in the world

We have also been ranked among the top 50 agriculture universities worldwide. (QS World University Rankings 2018/19)

Learn more about Lincoln

Our course completion rate for bachelor-level students is 90%

We offer students the skills they need to achieve their study goals. This is why nearly all of our bachelor-level students successfully complete their courses.

Learn more about Lincoln

We have one of the largest New Zealand art collections on public display

Our collection includes original artworks by New Zealand artists and the earliest piece is a Dutch etching dated 1639.
