Victoria’s researchers lead thinking

Demonising drug users

Why is cannabis use illegal but alcohol consumption encouraged in New Zealand? Researchers at Victoria are exploring our treatment of drugs and drug users.

Better drugs

Millions of people suffer from diseases for which there are no effective drugs. New treatments are being developed at Victoria for these unmet medical needs.

Memory and depression

Our recall of the past influences how we approach the present. But Victoria research shows memory can also be a direct window into our psychological wellbeing.

Our national and global rankings
